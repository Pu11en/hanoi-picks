The Bot Plan

Two specialized AI bots, both running on MiniMax, plus free built-in protection. Calm, minimal, hard to break.

The Setup — Two Bots + A Bouncer

Bot 1 · Auto, hands-off

Front Desk

● MiniMax · on Railway · 24/7
  • Welcomes every new member the moment they join
  • Answers the repeat questions: price, free vs premium, how to join
  • Quietly nudges free members toward Premium
  • Handles "contact us" so nobody gets ignored
Lives only in public rooms. Narrow job = barely hallucinates. Never sees the paid picks.
Bot 2 · You command it

Megaphone

● MiniMax · you trigger it
  • Posts announcements when you say so
  • Runs giveaways
  • Writes branded posts in your voice
  • Drops them into the right channel for you
Never chats with strangers — so it can't be tricked into spamming everyone. It only listens to you.
Not a bot we build

The Bouncer

● Discord AutoMod + a proven free mod bot
  • Blocks spam, scams, and bad links automatically
  • Battle-tested, purpose-built, zero cost
Building our own would just be a worse copy. Use what already works.

Why Two Bots, Not One

One mega-bot
Does everything
Gets confused · hallucinates more · one crash kills it all · risky power
→
Our split
Each bot, one job
Stays sharp · safer · one hiccup never takes the rest down

The big risk with merging: the Megaphone can blast your whole server. You never want the bot that chats with strangers to also hold that power. Separate = safe.

How We Build It

1

Stand up the Front Desk bot

New Discord app + token, separate from your existing bots. Zero risk to what's already working.

2

Give it the MiniMax brain

Wire MiniMax M3, send a test message, confirm it answers cleanly.

3

Set where it talks

Lock the channel map above — active, quiet, hands-off.

4

Give it a brain & a voice

Load the FAQ, pricing, free-vs-premium, and the calm brand voice + hard guardrails (never leak picks, no guaranteed-win claims).

5

Turn on the Bouncer

Switch on Discord AutoMod + add the free mod bot.

6

Add the Megaphone

Second MiniMax bot you command for announcements + giveaways, reading the brand style sheet.

7

Safety test, then go live

Cost cap, kill-switch, and a test with a second account before real members see it. Nothing ships until this passes.

Decisions To Lock

1 · Start with one bot or two?
Front Desk first, Megaphone later — or both together. Recommend: Front Desk first.
2 · How should the Front Desk reply?
Only when someone @mentions or DMs it, or jump in on its own. Recommend: mention/DM only — quiet, premium.
3 · How does it welcome people?
A private DM, a post in start-here, or DM with a fallback post. Recommend: post in start-here.
4 · Megaphone announcements
You approve every post before it goes out, or let it post on command. Recommend: you approve first.
