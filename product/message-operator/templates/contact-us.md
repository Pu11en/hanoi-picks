# Template — Contact Us

## Channel
contact-us

## Job
Give users clear support instructions and route issues to a human.

## Use for
- Payment help
- Proof submission help
- Access problems
- Confused users
- Partner signup questions

## Style
- Short
- Helpful
- Clear about what info to include

## Must include when relevant
- If it is free Premium proof, include the platform used.
- Say whether the user is in Free Chat or Premium.
- Do not promise instant access before proof is checked.

## Example
Drop your issue here and include what you’re trying to access.

If it’s free Premium proof, include the platform you signed up for and say whether you’re in Free Chat or Premium.
