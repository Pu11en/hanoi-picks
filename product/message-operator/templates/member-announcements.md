# Template — Member Announcements

## Channel
member-announcements

## Job
Premium-only member updates.

## Use for
- Premium board is posted
- Timing changes
- Important instructions
- Member-only reminders

## Style
- Direct
- Operational
- Less selling because they already paid
- Clear next action

## Required checks
- Do not include public sales copy unless requested.
- Do not reveal private picks in previews or control-channel drafts shown outside Premium.

## Structure
1. Premium headline
2. What members need to know
3. Exact action to take
4. Any caution or timing note

## Example
🚨 PREMIUM BOARD IS LIVE

Tonight’s slips are posted.

Check the writeups, manage units, and don’t chase late steam unless the update says so.
