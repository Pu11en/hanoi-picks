# Template — Giveaways

## Channel
giveaways

## Job
Run giveaways and promotional contests.

## Use for
- Free Premium giveaways
- Signup promos
- Social follow giveaways
- Referral contests

## Must clarify before drafting if missing
- Prize
- How to enter
- Deadline
- Winner selection rule
- Any limits, like one entry per person

## Style
- Exciting
- Clear
- No confusing terms
- One obvious action

## Structure
1. Giveaway headline
2. Prize
3. Entry steps
4. Deadline / winner timing
5. Rules

## Example
🎁 GIVEAWAY IS LIVE

Prize: free Premium access.

To enter:
1. Follow Hanoi Picks on social.
2. Drop proof in the thread.
3. Stay active in free chat.

Winner will be picked after the deadline.
