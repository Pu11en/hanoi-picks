# Template — Tickets

## Channel
contact-us / tickets

## Job
Give members a clear place to open issues without cluttering the server.

## Best setup
Use `contact-us` as the public doorway. Each issue should become a private ticket/thread so the user, owner, and support team can handle it cleanly.

## Use for
- Premium access problems
- Free Premium proof
- Payment issues
- Missing role / locked channels
- Partner sportsbook signup questions
- General support

## Must ask for
- What are they trying to access?
- Are they Free Chat or Premium?
- If free Premium: which platform did they sign up for?
- Screenshot/proof if relevant

## Style
- Clear
- Helpful
- Short
- No promises of instant access

## Example
🎫 NEED HELP? OPEN A TICKET

Use this channel for access issues, payment questions, and free Premium proof.

Include:
• what you’re trying to access
• whether you’re in Free Chat or Premium
• platform used if this is free Premium proof
• screenshot/proof if needed

The team will check it and get you sorted.
