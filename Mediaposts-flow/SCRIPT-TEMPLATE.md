# Hanoi Picks — Script Template (v1)

Locked from 4 reference reels (2 TikTok, 2 IG). Videos ~28–33 seconds, voiceover-driven parlay builds.

## The formula

Every video = **bold hook → 2–3 parlay legs → roll into the last leg.** Each leg follows the same shape: who, what line, why (recent form), and sometimes the matchup edge.

### 1. HOOK (1 line)
A bold, cocky claim that ties to the event/sport. Pick one vibe:
- "This is the official [EVENT] mad parlay that is almost guaranteed to hit."
- "Betting [SPORT/STAT] has been so free this year."
- "The [EVENT] is the easiest way to triple your [SPORT] betting."

### 2. LEG 1 — "starting off"
`Starting off with [PLAYER] to go over [LINE] [STAT].`
Then the proof:
`He's [averaging X / cleared this in N of his last 10 games].`

### 3. LEG 2 — "my second pick"
`My second pick is [PLAYER] to go over [LINE] [STAT].`
Proof + matchup angle:
`He's averaging [X] over his last 10 and is [hitting .300 / faces an opponent ranked 18th in [STAT] allowed].`

### 4. CLOSER LEG — "so we're going to roll with"
`My last guy [recorded X / cleared this in N of his last 10], so we're going to roll with [PLAYER] to go over [LINE] [STAT].`

## Confidence fillers (sprinkle in)
"This should be free." · "almost guaranteed" · "free pick" · "only needs one to catch this line."

## The voice
- First person, confident, fast. "We're going to roll with…"
- Always cite a recent-form number (last 10 games is the go-to).
- Lines are always half-points (0.5, 1.5, 5.5) → over/under props.
- No fluff intro, no signoff. Hook → picks → done.

## What I feed this template per video (the dump form)
- Sport / event (MLB, World Cup, etc.)
- 2–3 legs, each: player, stat type, line, recent-form stat, optional matchup stat
- Which hook vibe to open with

The bot fills the template from that dump, you edit it back-and-forth, then it's locked for the video.
