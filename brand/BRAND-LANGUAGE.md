---

## 1. The feeling (LOCKED)

Calm water, not loud noise. Intelligent and patient, not hype.
**Ancient wisdom + modern execution.** It whispers — that restraint is what makes it premium.

Most betting brands scream. Hanoi Picks is quiet, sure, and precise.

Words that ARE the brand: calm · water · flow · patience · reflection · hidden information · precision · signal.

Words to avoid: loud, hype, lock, guaranteed, flex, lambo, screaming.

---

## 2. The story

- **Hanoi** = a city built around water and rivers. Water is the through-line.
- **Personal brand.** The founder is the face — he WILL be shown (doxxed, on purpose). The brand follows a real person, not a faceless service.
- **Discord banner** = the founder near the water — modern Hanoi, fog, soft reflections, calm — created with AI.

Mood reference (from the client): a temple on a still lake, fog, a boat, the eye keeps returning to the water. "I want this to feel intelligent and calm, not loud."

## 3. Colors (LOCKED palette)

Muted, foggy, dark. Black + greys do the work. **One soft blue is the only real color.**
NOTE: pull exact codes off the client's Pinterest swatch — values below are close estimates, confirm before final.

| Name        | What it is                  | Approx | Use                          |
|-------------|-----------------------------|--------|------------------------------|
| Black Metal | near-black                  | `#0B0C0E` | background / base         |
| Cinder      | dark charcoal               | `#1C1E22` | panels, depth             |
| Chimney     | mid smoke grey              | `#6A6E74` | strokes, secondary text   |

## 4. The logo (structure LOCKED)

- An **HP monogram**. The **H and P must read instantly** — bold, geometric, clean. Luxury sports *syndicate* feel, NOT a puzzle mark.
- A thin **river / current line** woven in as negative space — a subtle secondary detail, never the main shape.
- **"HANOI PICKS"** set in small caps beneath the mark.
- **First deliverable:** the Discord server profile picture — round, simple, must work tiny.

**Still being explored:** the texture / style / finish (we find this from reference images), and the color ratio. Structure + palette stay fixed while we explore feel.

## 6. Bot voice rules

All Hanoi Picks bots use the same voice: calm, short, clear, and confident.

**Default posture:** helpful front desk, not hype salesman.

**Premium push level:** gentle nudge. Mention Premium when relevant, never force it.

Good:

> Free plays and updates are posted here. The full card lives in Premium when you’re ready.

Bad:

> Join Premium now for guaranteed locks 🔥🔥

Bot copy rules:

- Keep replies short.
- Use plain language.
- No emoji spam.
- No fake urgency.
- No guaranteed-win claims.
- No "lock," "guaranteed," "can't miss," "free money," or "100%."
- Always protect Premium picks and private channels.
- If unsure, escalate to the owner instead of inventing an answer.
